About Us

We make home cooking easier and healthier.

Our Mission

We believe that home cooking should be easy and enjoyable. Whether you are looking for meal inspiration, cooking on a budget, or finding ways to cook specific ingredients, DigiDish helps you hunt the recipe that you need.

Meet the Team

DigiDish is developed by a team of four food-loving students who enjoy home cooking experience and exploring great meals. Through DigiDish, we hope to inspire more people to explore new recipes, discover their hidden chef power, and enjoy food time with their families and friends.

Who use DigiDish?

DigiDish is designed for:

  • Students who are busy with labs and exams
  • Busy professionals who want to save time on meal planning
  • Families who want to cook together and involve all members
  • Food lovers who want to try new recipes and cuisines
  • Anyone looking for simple and stress-free cooking experience!

What DigiDish Offers

  • Recipe search based on ingredients
  • Budget-friendly meal suggestions
  • Preparation instruction and cooking time estimation
  • Resources and cooking tips
