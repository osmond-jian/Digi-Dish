Learn Cooking Basics

  1. Read the recipe first. Review ingredients and instructions before yous start.
  2. Prepare your ingredients. Measure, wash, and chop ingredients ahead of time.
  3. Use knife safely. Stree-free cooking starts from safety.
  4. Preheat your equipments.The right temperature is the key to good food texture.
  5. Season gradually. Taste as you cook!
  6. Clean as you go. Remember to turn off the heat!
