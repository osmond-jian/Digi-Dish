Cooking Resources

Here are some useful cooking resources to help you in the kitchen.

Learn Cooking Basics

  1. Read the recipe first. Review ingredients and instructions before you start.
  2. Prepare your ingredients. Measure, wash, and chop ingredients ahead of time.
  3. Use knives safely. Stress-free cooking starts from safety.
  4. Preheat your equipment. The right temperature is the key to good food texture.
  5. Season gradually. Taste as you cook!
  6. Clean as you go. Remember to turn off the heat!
